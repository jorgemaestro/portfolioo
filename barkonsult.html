Brief

The main goal was to redesign a responsive website for a local business in the surroundings of "El Matadero" where Ironhack-Madrid is actually located.

Out of the 3 options we collected; eventually, we decided to work with "Barkonsult". As we happened to have a quick but useful interview with one of the managers, we managed to gather all the information we actually needed to start working. An.

Barkonsult

They import, produce and distribute professional bar equipment and cocktail tools from all over the world. They've been in the business for over 30 years and today they are active in four markets - Sweden, Norway, Denmark and Spain. Barkonsult offers from bar tools and glasses, to drink mixers selected spirit brands and machines.

The Challenge

Improve the overall user experience of Barkonsult website. Optimizing their architecture information, user flows, payment methods and aesthetics in order to increase their sales while improving their public brand image.

Opportunities

Even though we started with a decent Desk research; surveys and interviews gave us the most valuable information. It was when we managed to talk with the owner of the very business when we were actually able to find out how their business was working and which weak points needed to be tackled and improved from our side.

Detected main areas of improvement:

  1. Site map and Information architecture needed to be optimized.
  2. Their well-known training courses were not visible anywhere in the site.
  3. There was not any rating section where people could see their “social proof”.
  4. Their payment method was confusing and barely worked most of the time.
  5. Their visuals didn’t dialog well according to their product. Barkonsult is actually a premium brand that provides one of the bests services and products of the city and still, their site wasn't conveying that message.

Competitive Analysis

Most of the options available in the market, did have features that our ecommerce didn't. We found out that Filters and Promotions were two of most important factors to implement. As both of them would have a direct impact in Barkonsult sales, we agreed that they were going to be two of the main areas to put the focus in.

Site Map

This was one of the most time consuming tasks we had faced so far. There're plenty of terms and names that only the experts know. That made it more challenging since we had to organize information that sometimes we didn't even know about. Lots of words were re-organized in different sections, some sections were removed and included into existing ones and some names were changed for easier and more intuitive ones.

We were forced to test a couple of main sections with potential users until we came up with the right name, since the existing ones seemed to confuse users. I personally realized how important testing is; no matter what phase of the project you are, testing and iterate seem to be foundations of any product development.

Their values and qualities must be recognized instantly.
