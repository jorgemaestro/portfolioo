The Challenge

User Journey

Research

x5

Ebooks market expected growth in the next 8 years.

85%

Think that the best is listening to stories while doing other activitives.

78%

Would read more if they were able to share the moment with others.

We identified in our surveys and interviews that most of people were missing the idea of being able to share their “reading” with someone the same way they do it when they consume platforms like Netflix.

A lot of readers feel like sharing their thoughts or their feelings after having read or listened to a story. It is precisely in the social act of sharing where we realized we had to put the focus on.

User Persona

“It feels better when I listen to my favourite book with someone. It’s like watching Netflix”

We all love stories but the way we consume them is absolutely different. Our user persona is used to read books on a daily basis. They tend to have an active social life and is not over his or her 40’s.

Problem Statement

“How might we transform the experience of listening to stories by one-self into something more social?”

Competitive Analysis

We learned that our app was actually, one of the best options in the market. The fact that it was such a complete application made it all this project a bit more challenging for us.

Brain Storming

After 5 minutes writting down anything that came out to our minds, standing up during the entire process in order to boost our ideas, we stumbled upon a few ideas that we knew they had certain potential.

Solution

Transform the very act of listening to stories, into the experience of sharing the moment with anyone anywhere in the world.

The Crazy 8

After 8 intense minutes we all proposed different ideas for every single screen. Once the task had finished we agreed on voting each screen so the "8" best proposals (out of the 24) took part of the whole project.
