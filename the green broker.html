Brief

Insurances & Finances

The first challenge was to figure out a problem statement related to one of these fields. Once we were able to spot it and after being approved by Ironhack, the goal was to find a quick solution in the form of a digital product.

After a couple of days listing quite a few problem statements, the final problem to be tackled sounded like so:

Most people actually want to improve their financial situation but they do not do anything about it.

Problem Statement

The next step was about defining the actual problem statement in order to know which direction to take. Answering questions like what, whom, where and why, would led me to a problem statemnt like the following one:

How might we encourage people to take action into their finances in order to improve their future responsibly?

User Journey

Research

Nowadays we all know that information has never been that reachable for everyone than today. However people are still making bad decisions or simply do not know what kind of decisions must be taken. Especially when it comes to their finances. Surveys and interviews played a key role when deciding which direction had to be taken.

  1. People don’t invest because of the lack knowledge they have.
  2. They think is too complicated to start.
  3. Most of people claim investing is just something for big fortunes.
  4. Lots of people do not even start because there are too many options to start with.

Empathy Map

We all love stories but the way we consume them is absolutely different. Our user persona is used to read books on a daily basis. They tend to have an active social life and is not over his or her 40’s.

“I barely know anything about finances. People say, it's a tricky world”

Competitive Analysis

We learned that our app was actually, one of the best options in the market. The fact that it was such a complete application made it all this project a bit more challenging for us.

Brainstorming

The direction was clear and the problem, pointed. Now, the challenge was about defining the "HOW". How to give solution to the problem? How to make people comfortable by investing their money and improving their future without being afraid about it? This tool was inspiring. By writting down these ideas, the solution came up almost instantly.

Goals

  1. Make investments accessible for everybody, designing an easy way through it.
  2. A place where most negative emotions disappear encouraging people to invest no matter the starting point.
  3. Making it easy to understand, taylormade and simplified are key factors in order to turn suspicion into real trust.

Principles

Defining the values of this product was essential. From now on, all UX and UI decisions would be based on this principles. Almost like this was a moodboard, the project started with this very image.
